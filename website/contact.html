Frequently Asked Questions

What should I expect in my first session?

Your first session will include a comprehensive fitness assessment, goal discussion, and a sample workout to get you started. We'll also review your program and answer any questions.

Do I need any equipment for remote training?

I can design programs for any equipment level, from bodyweight-only to full gym setups. We'll discuss your available equipment during our consultation.

How often should I train?

Training frequency depends on your goals, schedule, and fitness level. Most clients see great results with 2-3 sessions per week, but we'll create a plan that works for you.

What is your cancellation policy?

I require 24-hour notice for session cancellations. This allows me to offer the time slot to other clients and maintain a fair scheduling system.

Do you provide nutrition guidance?

Yes! I provide general nutrition guidance and meal planning support as part of my training services. For detailed meal plans, I can refer you to registered dietitians.

How do I get started?

Simply schedule a free consultation using the calendar above, or send me a message. We'll discuss your goals and determine the best training approach for you.
